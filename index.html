Sarjana Disko isn’t just a band, it’s a burst of retro energy created by a group of creative minds from Universitas Indonesia's Media Production 2024 class. Formed as a part of their final prorientation project, this six-member ensemble brings a fresh take on the discographic genre, blending groovy beats and nostalgic vibes to make audiences move.

Their debut single, "Get Into the Groove," showcases the band’s passion for connecting through music, combining upbeat rhythms with a lively music video that captures their unique style. Sarjana Disko is more than a project, it's a celebration of creativity, collaboration, and the joy of making music together.
